## 🇬🇧 About me

I'm a Research & Development Engineer with **10 years of experience** at the intersection of **3D, XR technologies, and Artificial Intelligence**.

My journey started with a **PhD** on how immersive technologies (VR/AR) can elicit transcendence experiences with cultural heritage — what I called *numinous heritage experiences*. Since then, I've been building tools and systems that sit at the crossroads of **technology and human impact**.

Currently at **Nfinite**, I design and deploy large-scale, production-ready pipelines combining generative AI, computer vision and 3D — from automated model training systems to image-to-3D pipelines.

🔭 Working on AI pipelines for audio dataset preparation and voice cloning  
🌱 Deepening expertise in LLMs, fine-tuning, and multimodal AI  
💬 Ask me about 3D, XR, Generative AI, ComfyUI, or Blender

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🎙️ [Audio Dataset Manager](https://github.com/maepopi/audio-dataset-manager)
An all-in-one tool to prepare audiobooks and large audio files for TTS and voice cloning pipelines. Built with Python and Gradio. ⭐ 12 stars

### 🎨 [Diffusers in ComfyUI](https://github.com/maepopi/Diffusers-in-ComfyUI)
A custom ComfyUI node enabling the use of the Diffusers pipeline directly inside ComfyUI. Available via ComfyUI Manager and Comfy Registry.

### 🤖 [LLM Trainer](https://github.com/maepopi/LLM_Trainer)
A Gradio app to quickly and easily fine-tune LLMs — built as a learning-by-doing project to understand how large language models work under the hood.
